Competitor Analysis Workflow

Analysis Framework:

Direct Competitors

Businesses offering similar products/services to the same market

Indirect Competitors

Alternative solutions addressing the same customer needs

Opportunity Gaps

Market spaces not adequately served by existing competitors

What to Analyze:

  • Pricing strategies and models
  • Marketing and branding approaches
  • Product/service features and quality
  • Customer reviews and feedback
  • Market positioning and messaging
  • Strengths and weaknesses

Pro Tip: Finding Your Competitive Advantage

Use the competitor map to identify gaps where you can position your family business. Look for intersections of high customer demand and low competitor saturation.

Research Best Practices

✅ Do's

  • • Ask specific, focused questions
  • • Cross-reference multiple data sources
  • • Pin important insights to your plans
  • • Update research regularly as markets change
  • • Share findings with your family team
  • • Validate AI insights with real-world data

❌ Don'ts

  • • Don't rely solely on AI research
  • • Don't ignore competitive threats
  • • Don't skip succession planning

  • • Don't overlook family dynamics
  • • Don't forget to document insights
